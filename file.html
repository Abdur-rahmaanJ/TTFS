Hi there, today I have a terrain-tool that might be useful for anyone who was ever unnerved by placing a larger number of deco parking lots before. YES! Your prayers have been answered! Now you can finally bring the progress of civilization into your cities and conveniently turn all the ugly and dirty landscape into neatly arranged parking lots. Parking lots as far as the eye can see... The tool works just like the trees from the terrain menu. You can draw out zones that are filled randomly with 1x1 parking lots or you can pick specific variants to build. The lots do not have any influence on anything. They are pure eye-candy. The graphics were for the most part originally made by Lobby and Theo. I just had the idea and compiled the various lots to form this tool. Most lots are (more or less) the same as the existing deco parking lots, but I added some new ones for the sake of variety. In total, this tool contains 17 different 1x1 lots (from A to Q).
